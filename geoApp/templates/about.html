About FRA Atlas

Comprehensive Forest Rights Analysis Platform for Sustainable Forest Management and Community Empowerment

Our Mission

FRA Atlas is dedicated to empowering forest-dwelling communities through comprehensive analysis of Forest Rights Act (FRA) claims, providing transparent data visualization, and facilitating informed decision-making for sustainable forest management and community development.

What We Do

Interactive Mapping

Advanced GIS mapping with real-time visualization of forest rights claims, village boundaries, and land use patterns using Leaflet.js technology.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive analysis of FRA claimants' eligibility for various government schemes including PM Kisan, MGNREGA, and other welfare programs.

Data Management

Secure storage and management of claimant data with JSON-based architecture ensuring data integrity and easy accessibility.

Government Integration

Seamless integration with government schemes and policies, providing eligibility analysis for various PM Yojanas and welfare programs.
