PM Ayushman Bharat Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is the world's largest health insurance scheme providing health coverage of ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization. It covers over 1,500 medical procedures and treatments across 25,000+ hospitals.

Coverage Amount: ₹5 lakh per family
Medical Procedures: 1,500+ treatments
Hospitals: 25,000+ empaneled

Eligibility Criteria for FRA Claimants

Rural Residence: Must be residing in rural areas (all FRA claimants qualify)
Economically Weaker Sections: Priority for small landholders and vulnerable groups
SC/ST Communities: High priority for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes
Women-headed Households: Special priority for female-headed families
Family Coverage: Entire family covered under single policy
