PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is a skill development initiative that aims to train youth in industry-relevant skills for better employment opportunities. The scheme provides free skill training, certification, and placement assistance to eligible candidates.

Training Duration: 150-300 hours
Age Group: 15-45 years
Placement Rate: 70%+

Eligibility Criteria for FRA Claimants

Age Requirement: Must be between 15-45 years (estimated based on claimant data)
Rural Residence: Must be residing in rural areas (all FRA claimants qualify)
Priority Groups: SC/ST, women, and economically weaker sections get priority
Skill Training: Industry-relevant skills with certification
Placement Support: Job placement assistance after training
