Startup India Overview

Startup India is a flagship initiative to build a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and startups in India. It aims to drive sustainable economic growth and generate large-scale employment opportunities through entrepreneurship development, innovation, and startup culture.

Innovation: Technology-driven solutions
Entrepreneurship: Business development
Employment: Job creation

Entrepreneurship Potential Criteria for FRA Claimants

Age Factor: Young adults (18-45 years) have higher startup potential
Land Resources: Landholding can be leveraged for agri-tech startups
Community Background: SC/ST communities get priority for startup support
Gender Inclusion: Women entrepreneurs get special startup incentives
Economic Need: Small landholders have higher motivation for entrepreneurship
